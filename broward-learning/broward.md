# Broward County Regulations

## Education Governance
- Broward County School Board – oversees public schools in the county
- School Board policies on enrollment, zoning, and transfers
- Magnet and choice programs unique to Broward

## Local Codes & Permitting
- Broward County Land Use Plan – zoning for educational facilities
- Building code requirements for schools and daycares
- Fire safety and ADA compliance for educational buildings
