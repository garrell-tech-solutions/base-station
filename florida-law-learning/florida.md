# Florida Law Overview

## Key Statutes
- Florida Constitution – framework for state governance
- Title XLVIII (K-20 Education Code) – governs public education
- Chapter 1002 – Student and parental rights in education
- Chapter 1003 – Public K-12 education requirements
- Chapter 1006 – Student discipline, safety, and welfare

## Recent Developments
- Expansion of school choice and voucher programs
- Updates to curriculum transparency requirements
- New parental notification laws
