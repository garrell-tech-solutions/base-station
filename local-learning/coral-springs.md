# Coral Springs / Local Education

## City Overview
- Population ~130,000; part of Broward County
- Multiple public schools within city limits (zoned under Broward County Schools)
- Active community involvement in education policy

## Education Requirements
- Florida compulsory education age: 6-16
- Options: public school, charter, private, homeschool
- Homeschool families must register with Broward County superintendent
- Annual evaluation required for homeschool students
